# BIR Filing Calendar

## Overview

This document provides a comprehensive calendar of Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR) filing deadlines for Philippine-based businesses. Deadlines are based on the National Internal Revenue Code (NIRC) as amended by the TRAIN Law (RA 10963), CREATE Law (RA 11534), and subsequent BIR Revenue Regulations.

**Important**: Filing deadlines that fall on a weekend or holiday are automatically moved to the next business day. Always verify with the latest BIR Revenue Memorandum Circulars (RMCs) for any deadline extensions.

---

## Monthly Filing Obligations

### Value Added Tax (VAT-Registered Taxpayers)

| Form | Description | Deadline | Who Must File |
|------|------------|----------|---------------|
| BIR Form 2550M | Monthly VAT Return | 25th of the following month | All VAT-registered taxpayers |
| | | | Exception: Returns for months ending a quarter (March, June, September, December) are filed quarterly via 2550Q |

**Example Schedule for Q1:**
- January VAT: File by February 25
- February VAT: File by March 25
- March VAT: Filed as part of Q1 quarterly return (2550Q)

### Withholding Taxes

| Form | Description | Deadline | Who Must File |
|------|------------|----------|---------------|
| BIR Form 1601-C | Monthly Remittance of Withholding Tax on Compensation | 10th of the following month | All employers |
| BIR Form 0619-E | Monthly Remittance of Expanded Withholding Tax | 10th of the following month | All withholding agents |
| BIR Form 0619-F | Monthly Remittance of Final Withholding Tax | 10th of the following month | All withholding agents with FWT obligations |

**Note on eFPS Filers**: Taxpayers mandated to use the Electronic Filing and Payment System (eFPS) have a 5-day extension on the deadline. For example, 1601-C due on the 10th becomes the 15th for eFPS filers.

### Percentage Tax (Non-VAT Registered)

| Form | Description | Deadline | Who Must File |
|------|------------|----------|---------------|
| BIR Form 2551M | Monthly Percentage Tax Return | 20th of the following month | Non-VAT registered taxpayers with gross sales/receipts not exceeding PHP 3,000,000 |

## Quarterly Filing Obligations

### Value Added Tax

| Form | Description | Deadline | Period Covered |
|------|------------|----------|----------------|
| BIR Form 2550Q | Quarterly VAT Return | 25th of the month following the quarter end | Q1: Jan-Mar, Q2: Apr-Jun, Q3: Jul-Sep, Q4: Oct-Dec |

**Quarterly VAT Filing Schedule:**
- Q1 (Jan-Mar): Due April 25
- Q2 (Apr-Jun): Due July 25
- Q3 (Jul-Sep): Due October 25
- Q4 (Oct-Dec): Due January 25 of the following year

### Withholding Tax Remittances

| Form | Description | Deadline | Period Covered |
|------|------------|----------|----------------|
| BIR Form 1601-EQ | Quarterly Remittance of Expanded Withholding Tax | Last day of the month following the quarter end | Same as VAT quarters |
| BIR Form 1601-FQ | Quarterly Remittance of Final Withholding Tax | Last day of the month following the quarter end | Same as VAT quarters |

**Quarterly WT Filing Schedule:**
- Q1: Due April 30
- Q2: Due July 31
- Q3: Due October 31
- Q4: Due January 31 of the following year

### Quarterly Income Tax

| Form | Description | Deadline | Period Covered |
|------|------------|----------|----------------|
| BIR Form 1702Q | Quarterly Income Tax Return (Corporations) | 60 days after quarter end | Q1: Jan-Mar, Q2: Apr-Jun, Q3: Jul-Sep |

**Note**: No quarterly return is filed for Q4. The annual income tax return covers the full year including Q4.

**Quarterly ITR Filing Schedule:**
- Q1: Due May 30 (60 days after March 31)
- Q2: Due August 29 (60 days after June 30)
- Q3: Due November 29 (60 days after September 30)

## eFPS Mandate

The following taxpayers are required to use the Electronic Filing and Payment System (eFPS):

1. Large taxpayers registered with the BIR Large Taxpayer Service
2. Top 20,000 private corporations (by tax payments)
3. Top 5,000 individual taxpayers
4. Taxpayers mandated by their Revenue District Office (RDO)
5. Government-owned and controlled corporations (GOCCs)
6. Cooperatives registered with the Cooperative Development Authority (CDA)

**eFPS Benefits:**
- 5-day extension on filing deadlines
- Electronic payment processing
- Automated acknowledgment receipt
- Accessible at efps.bir.gov.ph

**Non-eFPS Filing Options:**
- eBIRForms (offline preparation, online submission)
- Manual filing at the RDO (for authorized filers)

## Penalties for Late Filing

| Violation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| Late filing | 25% surcharge on tax due |
| Failure to file | 50% surcharge on tax due |
| Late payment | 12% annual interest (deficiency interest) |
| Late payment | 25% surcharge on tax due |
| Compromise penalty | Varies by violation (per RMO 7-2015) |

**Statute of Limitations:**
- Assessment: 3 years from date of filing (or date due, whichever is later)
- Fraudulent return: 10 years from discovery
- No return filed: 10 years from date due

## Odoo Integration Notes

### Automated Reminders

The platform can be configured to send automated reminders via Slack and email:

- **7 days before deadline**: Warning notification to the accounting team
- **3 days before deadline**: Urgent notification to the Finance Manager
- **1 day before deadline**: Critical alert to CFO

These are configured via n8n workflows connected to the BIR filing calendar.

### Tax Computation in Odoo

The following BIR computations are automated in the Odoo platform:

- **VAT Output/Input**: Automatically computed on invoices and bills based on tax configuration
- **Expanded Withholding Tax**: Applied to vendor payments via fiscal positions and tax groups
- **Compensation Withholding**: Computed by the payroll module based on tax tables
- **Quarterly ITR**: Summarized from the income statement accounts

### Report Generation

Odoo can generate the supporting data for BIR forms:

| BIR Form | Odoo Report | Module |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| 2550M/Q | Tax Balance Report filtered by VAT accounts | `account_tax_balance` |
| 1601-C | Payroll tax summary | HR Payroll |
| 1601-EQ | EWT ledger by payee and tax type | Custom report |
| 1702 | Income statement + tax adjustments | `account_financial_report` + `mis_builder` |
| 2307 | Withholding tax certificate per vendor | Custom print |
| 2316 | Annual compensation certificate per employee | HR Payroll |

**Note**: The actual BIR forms must be filed via eFPS or eBIRForms. Odoo generates the data; the filing is done on the BIR platform.
