# Investor Offer Benefits — canonical list

## Azure
- Starts $100,000 credits (max varies by investor — up to $150k observed in docs; lifetime cap $350k)
- Serverless GPUs + on-demand GPU VMs
- 1:1 Azure technical experts (Azure advisory, pairing sessions)
- Azure Standard Support free
- GTM support (from value-prop refinement to selling through Microsoft)

## Product seats (12 months from activation)
| Product | Seats/Users |
|---|---|
| M365 Business Premium | 50 |
| Dynamics 365 Customer Service Enterprise | 10 |
| Dynamics 365 Sales Enterprise | 10 |
| Dynamics 365 Team Members | 25 |
| Power Apps | 10 |
| Power Automate | 10 |
| Power BI | 10 |
| GitHub Enterprise | 20 |
| Visual Studio Enterprise | 5 |

## What's NOT included
- M365 Copilot (separate purchase)
- Enterprise-tier OpenAI quotas (invitation-only, tied to Pegasus / $25k+ tier)

## Gotchas
- "Discounts on products you know and love" — text says "free" for the product table, but headline says "discounts" — ambiguous wording; treat the table as free for the specified seat counts during the 12-month window
- Billing country must match credit-card country
- Each benefit has its own terms; 12-month window starts on activation not approval
