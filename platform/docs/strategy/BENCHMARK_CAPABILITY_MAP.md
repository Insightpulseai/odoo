# BENCHMARK_CAPABILITY_MAP

> Version: 1.0.0
> Last updated: 2026-03-14
> Canonical repo: `platform`

## Purpose

Map external benchmark products and platforms to the target InsightPulseAI capability model. This is a cross-offering, cross-industry capability benchmark anchoring product strategy for all offerings.

## Benchmark Sources

- Databricks Financial Services
- Databricks Retail
- Databricks Media & Entertainment
- Databricks Apps
- Smartly
- Quilt.AI
- LIONS Marketing Assistant
- DataIntelligence.ro

## Benchmark-to-Capability Model

### Databricks

Use as benchmark for:
- Unified data and AI platform
- Governed industry intelligence
- Internal secure data/AI applications (Databricks Apps)
- Financial services analytics (hyperpersonalization, fraud, risk, compliance, treasury)
- Retail intelligence (customer insights, marketing growth, engagement, retail media)
- Media/audience/monetization intelligence (churn, content supply chain, ad inventory)

### Smartly

Use as benchmark for:
- Creative operations
- Campaign execution orchestration
- Cross-channel media management
- Marketing intelligence in execution flow
- AI-scaled creative production

### Quilt.AI

Use as benchmark for:
- Consumer intelligence
- Cultural intelligence
- Segmentation
- Brand health
- Creative testing
- Social/search/web discourse analysis

### LIONS Marketing Assistant

Use as benchmark for:
- Evidence-grounded strategic assistance
- Case-study-backed recommendations
- Trusted source retrieval
- Cross-category inspiration
- Client-defensible rationale

### DataIntelligence.ro

Use as benchmark for:
- Real-time marketing BI
- Real-time dashboarding
- Campaign/digital ecosystem monitoring
- Data hub for marketing growth

## Target InsightPulseAI Offering Implications

### Odoo on Cloud

System of action for:
- ERP, CRM, finance, inventory, projects, approvals
- Multi-entity workflow execution
- Operational control

### Odoo Copilot

Guided operating copilot for:
- Informational support
- Navigational assistance
- Bounded transactional assistance
- Evidence-grounded recommendations
- Role-aware workflow support

### Analytics

Cross-domain intelligence layer for:
- Account/customer 360
- Campaign/media performance
- Retail/customer growth
- Financial risk/compliance/decisioning
- Executive visibility

### Automations

Execution/orchestration layer for:
- Approvals
- Campaign/content workflows
- Task routing
- Exception handling
- Event-driven actions

### Managed Operations

Reliability/governance layer for:
- Deployment
- Monitoring
- Upgrades
- Release control
- Support operations

## Non-Goals

- Do not copy vendor UX or product taxonomy verbatim
- Do not reposition Odoo as the intelligence platform
- Do not reposition Databricks-like intelligence as the operational system of action

## Canonical Product Doctrine

| Layer | Role |
|-------|------|
| Odoo | System of action |
| Copilot | Guided operating layer |
| Analytics | Intelligence layer |
| Automations | Orchestration layer |
