# PRD: Microsoft AI-Led ERP Practice — InsightPulseAI

## 1. Problem Statement
InsightPulseAI needs a disciplined motion to capitalize on the Microsoft ecosystem. Generic "Dynamics selling" is ineffective. We need a specific, repeatable practice-build motion that combines AI transformation with service-centric ERP delivery.

## 2. Goals
- Build a service-centric ERP practice anchored on **Project Operations**.
- Define and package two high-propensity FY26 scenarios.
- Establish a "Vertical Wedge" for initial market entry.
- Identify and close the CRM + ERP fusion skill gaps.

## 3. Practice Scenarios & Commercialization (Requirements)

### 3.1 COMMERCIALIZE: D365 CSP Incentive Strategy (FY26)
- **Requirement**: Operationalize the FY26 D365 CSP incentive structure: 4.0% Core, 7.00% F&S Strategic (Tier 1), 8.00% BC Strategic (Tier 2), and 7.50% Growth Accelerator.
- **Accepted Criteria**: Profitability model distinguishes Core/Strategic/Growth; internal handling remains confidential.

### 3.2 COMMERCIALIZE: ERP Transformation with AI Offer
- **Requirement**: Package the technical capabilities of **Pulser for Odoo** ([prd.md](file:///Users/tbwa/Documents/GitHub/Insightpulseai/spec/pulser-odoo/prd.md)) and **Record to Report** ([prd.md](file:///Users/tbwa/Documents/GitHub/Insightpulseai/spec/pulser-record-to-report/prd.md)) into a Microsoft-aligned "FY26 ERP Transformation" offer.
- **Accepted Criteria**: Offer statement maps technical features to business outcomes (accuracy, close speed, compliance); target buyers defined.

### 3.3 COMMERCIALIZE: 2-Tier / "Surround the Incumbents" Scenario
- **Requirement**: Package the agility of Odoo on Azure for subsidiaries, grounded in the **Operations** ([prd.md](file:///Users/tbwa/Documents/GitHub/Insightpulseai/spec/odoo-on-azure-operations/prd.md)) and **Finance** baselines.
- **Accepted Criteria**: ICP maps subsidiary agility to Dynamics 365; trigger events and competitive incumbents defined.

### 3.4 COMMERCIALIZE: AI and Agent Transformation Scenario
- **Requirement**: Define the AI-attach motion using **Copilot Studio** and **Pulser Agents** to drive tenant revenue growth.
- **Accepted Criteria**: Success metrics distinguish Microsoft Copilot (informational) from Pulser Agents (transactional); aligns to [Foundry Delegation](file:///Users/tbwa/Documents/GitHub/Insightpulseai/docs/contracts/FOUNDRY_DELEGATION_CONTRACT.md).

### 3.5 BUILD PRACTICE: Internal Delivery Kernel (Project Operations)
- **Requirement**: Adopt the **Project to Profit** ([prd.md](file:///Users/tbwa/Documents/GitHub/Insightpulseai/spec/pulser-project-to-profit/prd.md)) operating model for InsightPulseAI's own delivery practice (Bid-to-Billing).
- **Accepted Criteria**: Internal practice uses Pulser P2P for WBS, margin, and milestone control; internal profitability dashboard operational.

### 3.6 COMMERCIALIZE: Tenant-Level CSP Growth Playbook
- **Requirement**: Build playbook around YoY monthly billed revenue growth per tenant, utilizing AI-attach and seat expansion.
- **Accepted Criteria**: Modeled at tenant level; scenarios include new customer, seat expansion, and partner-switch.

### 3.7 GOVERN: Business Applications Incentive Compliance
- **Requirement**: Maintain eligibility under FY26 rules ($25K PLA for Indirect; Designation Score Monitoring).
- **Accepted Criteria**: Designation and revenue-thresholds tracked; 5-month capability-score window monitored monthly.

### 3.8 FINOPS: Market B Assumptions (Philippines-Led)
- **Requirement**: Use Market B as the default incentive assumption for all PH-led motions.
- **Accepted Criteria**: Market B used for all PH payout modeling; Oct 2025 shift and recalculation timing modeled.

### 3.9 COMMERCIALIZE: Marketplace Differentiation (Odoo)
- **Requirement**: Establish positioning that rejects commodity Odoo VM hosting in favor of AI-First Analytics and Compliance.
- **Accepted Criteria**: “Not our lane” statement defined for raw hosting; differentiation proof required before publishing.

### 3.10 GOVERN: Marketplace Gatekeeping
- **Requirement**: Enforce a differentiation gate before any marketplace listing is published.
- **Accepted Criteria**: “Go / no-go” gate defined; value proof required and verified against the [V0.6 Platform baseline](file:///Users/tbwa/Documents/GitHub/Insightpulseai/spec/odoo-on-azure/prd.md).
