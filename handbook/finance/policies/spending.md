# How to Spend Company Money

## Core Principle

**Spend company money like it's your own money.**

Use good judgment, be frugal, and always consider whether the expense is necessary and reasonable.

## Approval Thresholds (PHP)

| Amount | Approval Required | Turnaround |
|--------|-------------------|------------|
| < ₱5,000 | Self-approval with receipt | Immediate |
| ₱5,000 - ₱25,000 | Manager approval | 1-2 business days |
| ₱25,000 - ₱100,000 | Department Head + Finance | 2-3 business days |
| > ₱100,000 | CFO/Finance Director | 3-5 business days |

## Expense Categories

### Always Reimbursable

- **Business travel** (economy class, reasonable hotels)
- **Client entertainment** (with prior approval, business purpose documented)
- **Professional development** (courses, certifications with approval)
- **Office supplies** (reasonable quantities)
- **Software subscriptions** (from approved list)
- **Communication** (mobile data for business use)

### Requires Pre-Approval

- International travel
- Equipment purchases > ₱10,000
- Event hosting
- Gifts to clients/vendors
- Training programs

### Never Reimbursable

- Personal travel
- Alcohol (except documented client entertainment)
- Traffic violations or parking fines
- Personal phone bills (unless on-call role)
- Entertainment without business purpose
- First-class travel (without CFO approval)

## Receipt Requirements

| Amount | Documentation Required |
|--------|----------------------|
| < ₱1,000 | Any receipt or acknowledgment |
| ₱1,000 - ₱5,000 | Official Receipt (OR) |
| > ₱5,000 | OR + Purchase Order/Contract |

### What Makes a Valid Receipt

- Date of transaction
- Vendor/merchant name
- Itemized list of goods/services
- Amount including VAT (if applicable)
- OR number (for amounts > ₱1,000)

## Reimbursement Process

1. **Capture receipt** via Odoo mobile app or email
2. **Categorize expense** (meals, transport, supplies, etc.)
3. **Add business justification** (who, what, why)
4. **Submit for approval** (auto-routed based on amount)
5. **Receive reimbursement** on next cycle

### Reimbursement Schedule

- **15th of month**: Expenses submitted by 10th
- **30th of month**: Expenses submitted by 25th

## Corporate Card Usage

If issued a corporate card:

- Use for business expenses only
- Submit receipts within 5 business days
- Report lost/stolen cards immediately
- See [Corporate Card Policy](cards.md) for details
