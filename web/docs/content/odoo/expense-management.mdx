# Expense Management

Expense management in Odoo on Cloud streamlines how employees submit, approve, and reimburse business expenses. It connects to projects, analytic accounts, and tax workflows — so every expense is properly coded, approved, and visible in financial reporting.

## Key Capabilities

### Employee Expense Submission
- Mobile-friendly expense entry (photo receipt → OCR extraction)
- Categorized by expense type (travel, meals, supplies, telecom, etc.)
- Multi-currency support with auto-conversion
- Per-diem and mileage calculation
- Attach receipts (photo, PDF, email forward)

### OCR & Document Intelligence
- Azure Document Intelligence extracts: merchant, date, amount, tax, category
- Auto-fills expense fields from receipt photo
- Confidence scoring — flags low-confidence extractions for manual review
- Supports Philippine receipts (BIR Official Receipts, VAT invoices)

### Approval Workflow
- Configurable approval chain: direct manager → department head → finance
- Threshold-based routing: < PHP 5,000 auto-approve, > PHP 50,000 requires CFO
- Delegation support (approver out of office → delegate)
- Reject with reason, return for revision
- Approval SLA tracking (target: 48 hours)

### Reimbursement
- Batch payment processing
- Direct bank transfer or payroll deduction
- Multi-company reimbursement
- Currency gain/loss handling for foreign expenses

### Policy Enforcement
- Expense limits by category (e.g., meals: PHP 1,500/day max)
- Duplicate detection (same vendor + date + amount)
- Weekend/holiday flagging
- Receipt required above threshold
- Blocked categories (e.g., alcohol, entertainment caps)

## Expense-to-Project Flow

1. Employee submits expense → selects project
2. Expense coded to project's analytic account
3. After approval → journal entry created
4. Cost appears in project P&L and PPM dashboard
5. Reimbursement processed via AP

## FAQ

**Q: Can employees submit expenses from their phone?**
A: Yes. The Odoo mobile app lets employees photograph receipts and submit expenses on the go. OCR auto-extracts the details.

**Q: How does expense OCR work?**
A: Azure Document Intelligence (our `data-intel-ph` resource) processes receipt images, extracts structured data (merchant, date, amount, tax), and pre-fills the expense form. Employees review and confirm.

**Q: Are foreign currency expenses supported?**
A: Yes. Employees enter the original currency amount. Odoo converts using the daily rate from `currency_rate_update` (OCA module) and tracks FX gain/loss on reimbursement.

**Q: How are expenses linked to BIR tax filings?**
A: Deductible expenses with VAT flow into the input VAT computation for 2550M. The 50% deductibility rule for meals and entertainment is enforced at the GL level.
