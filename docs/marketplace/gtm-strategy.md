## Campaign Tracking (Mandatory for All Marketing)

Every URL linking to the marketplace listing must include:

```
?ocid={campaign_id}
&utm_source={source}      # website, linkedin, facebook, email
&utm_medium={medium}      # email, social, ppc, organic
&utm_campaign={campaign}  # spring_launch, bir_compliance, tbwa_pilot
&utm_term={keyword}       # odoo_finance, bir_2307, month_end_close
&utm_content={element}    # cta_button, hero_banner, sidebar_link
```

Track effectiveness in Partner Center → Insights workspace.
Leads with campaign tags appear in Partner Center → Referrals workspace.
