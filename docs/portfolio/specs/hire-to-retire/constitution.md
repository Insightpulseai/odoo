# Hire-to-Retire Process Constitution

## Non-Negotiables

### 1. DOLE Statutory Compliance (Philippines)
- **Final Pay**: ≤30 calendar days from separation (Labor Advisory No. 06-20)
- **COE**: ≤3 calendar days from employee request
- **Pay Frequency**: At least 2x per month (≤16 day interval)
- **Leave Credit Conversion**: Required in final pay computation

### 2. Internal SLA (TBWA\SMP Champion Standard)
- **Final Pay**: ≤7 calendar days from Clearance Completed
- **First Pay**: ≥7 days worked before payroll cutoff
- **Clearance**: Complete within 5 business days of LWD

### 3. Process Ownership
- **Primary Owner**: Finance Shared Services / Controlling
- **Co-Owner**: HR Director / HRBP Lead
- **Escalation Only**: COO / MD (SLA breach, high-value approvals)

### 4. RACI Accountability
- Every task must have: Preparer, Reviewer, Approver
- No task can be self-approved
- Audit trail required for all state transitions

### 5. Holiday-Aware Scheduling
- Philippine holidays must be excluded from workday calculations
- Weekend days (Sat/Sun) excluded from business day counts
- Calendar maintained by HR Ops

## Quality Gates

1. All clearance signatures collected before final pay calculation
2. Leave balance verified by HR before conversion
3. Outstanding advances verified by Finance before final pay
4. Tax computation reviewed by Finance before release
5. COE template approved by Legal
