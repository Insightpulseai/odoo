Official DOLE Guidance

"The final pay shall be released within thirty (30) days from the date of separation or termination of employment, unless a more favorable company policy, individual/collective agreement, or company practice exists." - DOLE Labor Advisory No. 06-20, Series of 2020

Note: TBWA\SMP's internal 7-day SLA qualifies as a "more favorable company practice" and exceeds the statutory requirement.

Unused Leave Credit Conversion Formula

Vacation Leave (VL)

VL Conversion = VL Balance x Daily Rate
Daily Rate = Monthly Basic / 22 work days

Example: 5 VL days x (P50,000 / 22) = P11,363.64

Sick Leave (SL)

SL Conversion = SL Balance x Daily Rate
(If company policy allows SL conversion)

Example: 3 SL days x (P50,000 / 22) = P6,818.18

Total Leave Conversion (Example):
VL (P11,363.64) + SL (P6,818.18) = P18,181.82
