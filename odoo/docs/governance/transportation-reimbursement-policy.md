# Policy on Transportation Reimbursements

> **Effective Date**: April 1, 2026
> **Classification**: Confidential - Not for Public Consumption or Distribution
> **Plane Wiki**: To be published as wiki page in Plane workspace

---

TBWA employees occasionally need to travel to client sites in performing their
duties. TBWA undertakes to reimburse its employees for actual travel costs
incurred to and from client sites or any offsite location.

## Allowed Modes of Transportation

Employees may travel to and from client sites or offsite locations using the
following:

- Taxi
- Ride-hailing services (Premium is **not allowed**)
- Premium Point-to-Point Bus
- Airplane (Economy Class only; Business Class is **not allowed**)
- If an employee chooses to use a personal vehicle, reimbursement will be based
  solely on the **equivalent GrabCar fare**, subject to documentation
  requirements below.

## General Rules for Reimbursement

1. All reimbursements must:
   a. Be **official / business-related**
   b. Be **approved** based on grant of authority
   c. Be supported by **complete and valid documentation** (email proof of
      meeting or calendar meeting schedule)

2. Reimbursements must be filed within **30 days** from the date the expense was
   incurred. If this is exceeded, you may not be reimbursed in full.

3. Use the itemized expense report form to claim reimbursements.

4. Approval authority:
   a. Up to **PHP 10,000** - Department Head / Managing Director and Finance
      Director
   b. Above **PHP 10,000** - COO and Finance Director

## Documentation Requirements

### STRICTLY NO RECEIPT / NO VALID SCREENSHOT, NO REIMBURSEMENT

### A. Public Transportation

- Official receipt, or
- Emailed receipt (Ride-hailing services or Airline), or
- Screenshot from the application **taken at the time of incurrence**, not at
  the time of reimbursement

Screenshots must be:
- Clear and readable
- Showing **date, time, route, and fare**

### B. Personal Vehicle

While employees may choose to use their own vehicle, TBWA will **not reimburse
actual fuel, mileage, depreciation or parking fee**.

- Reimbursement will be capped at the equivalent **GrabCar 4-Seater fare** for
  the same route.
- Fare reference must be generated via the **Grab App Fare Estimate**.
- Toll fee may be subjected for approval depending on the situation or location
  of meeting.

#### Fare Estimate Screenshot Requirement

To ensure accuracy and fairness, the screenshot must:

- Be taken from the **Grab App**
- Reflect the **actual date and approximate time** of travel
- Clearly show:
  - Pickup and drop-off locations
  - Estimated fare range
  - Date and time visible on the device screen

> Screenshots taken on a different date or time will not be honored, as Grab
> fares vary based on demand and timing.

## Travel From / To Home

If employees travel directly from home to the client site or vice versa,
reimbursement will be capped at the equivalent GrabCar fare **from the TBWA
Office to the client site**, based on a compliant Grab app screenshot.

## Group Travel

- Employees are expected to act responsibly.
- When traveling as a group, **ride together** whenever possible.
- Only **one transportation reimbursement** may be claimed per trip.
- This is a reimbursement policy, not an allowance.

## Overtime Transportation

Please refer to OT policy.

## Finance Review and Compliance

1. Finance reserves the right to:
   - Question expenses that appear excessive or unreasonable
   - Reject claims that do not meet documentation or approval requirements

2. For the avoidance of doubt:
   - Estimated mileage-based reimbursements are **no longer allowed**
   - Parking fees are **not reimbursable**
