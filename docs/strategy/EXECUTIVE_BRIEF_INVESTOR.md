# InsightPulseAI

## What it is

InsightPulseAI is building a self-hosted enterprise platform on Azure that combines ERP, governed intelligence, and agentic AI into one extensible operating stack.

## Core architecture

- **Odoo CE 18** — transactional system of record
- **Azure Databricks** — governed intelligence layer
- **Azure AI Foundry** — agent and copilot runtime
- **Azure DevOps + GitHub** — delivery and control plane

## Why it matters

The platform is designed to replace expensive enterprise SaaS categories with a lower-cost, owned, governable stack that can be extended through agents while still targeting enterprise-grade maturity.

## Target markets / solution families

- Customer & Identity Intelligence
- Campaign Planning / Measurement / Optimization
- Brand, Cultural & Creative Intelligence
- AI Visibility / GEO Intelligence
- Revenue & Campaign Copilots inside ERP

## Business model thesis

Own the platform, reduce recurring SaaS dependency, and package repeatable vertical solutions across:

- Marketing
- Retail / commerce
- Media and entertainment
- Finance / revenue assurance
- AI copilots and agent workflows

## Delivery model

- Spec-driven SDLC

- OCA-first ERP extension strategy
- Self-hosted-first operating posture
