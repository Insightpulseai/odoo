# Key facts

- Subscription type: Azure Sponsorship (MS-AZR-0036P)
- Can't be used for: Microsoft Support plans, third-party branded products, Marketplace purchases, products sold separately from Azure
- Can't be combined with other licensing/prepaid/credit offers
- Can't be shared or merged across accounts
- Can't transfer between MSAs
- Credits auto-convert to pay-as-you-go when exhausted or expired
- Prohibited uses: crypto mining, DoS, spam, illegal activity

## Eligibility errors
- Work/school accounts are rejected → switch to MSA
- Existing Azure subscription on the MSA → ineligible

## What breaks the verification path
- Not within 90-day window
- Already verified or denied
- Incomplete registration info

## Contrast with investor offer (per same page FAQ)
"If your startup is affiliated with the Microsoft for Startups Investor Network, you may be eligible for a higher level of support... including increased Azure credits"
