```text
- not a gym, not a co-working office, not a nightclub

- lounge area

- industrial shell upgraded into a clean modern studio

Avoid:
- fitness equipment, yoga mats, treadmills, trainers / gym instructors
- neon nightclub visuals
- generic podcast bedroom setups
- residential interiors
- fake luxury mansion look
- oversaturated colors, clutter
- cheap influencer set aesthetics

Brand mood:
- serious creative production
- modern recording studio
- warehouse conversion
- practical + premium
- editorial, architectural, cinematic
```
