# Microsoft Marketplace: GTM Strategy — InsightPulseAI

## Objective
Commercialize the IPAI platform on Azure Marketplace through Partner Center, leveraging Microsoft's global distribution and consolidated billing.

## Commercialization Lanes

### Lane 1: Professional Services (Fastest)
- **Offer Type**: Consulting Service / Managed Service.
- **Value**: Governed Agent Factory rollout, Odoo-on-Azure modernization, and TaxPulse-PH compliance automation.
- **Target**: Enterprise finance teams needing AI governance.

### Lane 2: SaaS / AI Agent Offer
- **Offer Type**: SaaS.
- **Value**: `ipai-odoo-copilot-azure` productized with tiered pricing.
- **Target**: Existing Odoo users on Azure needing a governed copilot.

### Lane 3: Azure Managed Application
- **Offer Type**: Azure Application.
- **Value**: Turnkey "Odoo.sh on Azure" platform with ACA, Front Door, and V2 Governance built-in.
- **Target**: Partners and large enterprises building their own agentic ERP fleets.

## Partner Center Roadmap
1. **Enrollment**: Set up Partner Center account and Tax/Payout profiles.
2. **Offer Creation**: Draft the "IPAI Finance Automation Services" offer.
3. **Technical Integration**: Define SaaS fulfillment APIs (for Lane 2).
4. **Publishing**: Submit for certification and go-live.

## Success Metrics
- Marketplace Leads (MQLs).
- Consolidated Billing Volume (MACC decrement).
- Agent Factory V2 Adoption Rate.
