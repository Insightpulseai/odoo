# Constitution: Microsoft AI-Led ERP Practice — InsightPulseAI

## 1. Objective
Establish a world-class, service-centric ERP practice on Microsoft Dynamics 365, specifically targeting the intersection of Finance/Operations and AI Agent transformation.

## 2. Core Doctrines

### 1. Project Operations as the Kernel
The practice is anchored on **Dynamics 365 Project Operations**. This is not just a tool, but the internal operating engine for the entire bid-to-billing lifecycle. All IPAI delivery capability must flow through a Project Operations-governed rhythm.

### 2. CRM + ERP Fusion
We reject the siloed approach to Business Applications. A Project Operations practice requires unified capability across CRM (Customer Engagement) and ERP (Finance). Every team member must understand the "Project Lifecycle" from sales resourcing to financial close.

### 3. Industry Wedge First
Broad horizontal sprawl is the enemy of quality. We choose a narrow starting wedge (e.g., Professional Services or specific Industry) and build deep repeatability there before expanding.

### 4. AI-Native Delivery
We don't just sell AI; we use it. Every "repeatable" task in the practice lifecycle is a candidate for an IPAI Agent. We build light IP (dashboards, accelerators, agent tools) into every client engagement.

### 5. Incentive Design Authority
Every deal must be evaluated through the **FY26 CSP/MCI Incentive Model**: 4.0% Core, 7.00% Finance/SC Tier 1, 8.00% Business Central Tier 2, and 7.50% Growth Accelerator. We prioritize the highest-margin motions (Business Central Strategic Accelerator) and drive **tenant-level YoY growth** through AI-attach (Copilot Studio) and seat expansion.

### 6. Eligibility Guardianship
Incentive eligibility is a first-class business risk. We proactively monitor the **5-month-plus-current-month capability-score window** (25-point minimum) and trailing 12-month revenue thresholds ($1M for Direct, $25K for Indirect). Loss of eligibility is treated as a severe delivery failure.

### 7. Market B Economics (Philippines)
Unless explicitly stated otherwise, all local-led motions are modeled using **Market B** assumptions as defined in the MCI guide. Philippines-led engagements must align to Market B rate cards for all incentive planning and yield forecasting.



## 3. Targeted Scenario Scopes

### Scenario A: 2-Tier "Surround the Incumbent"
- **Target**: Large enterprises with legacy SAP/Oracle at HQ.
- **Motion**: Deploy Dynamics 365 at subsidiaries, new entities, or M&A integrations.
- **Value**: Agile, lower-cost ERP that integrates with the HQ system of record via IPAI bridges.

### Scenario B: AI and Agent Transformation
- **Target**: Existing Dynamics or Odoo customers.
- **Motion**: Layer IPAI Agents and Microsoft Copilot over the transactional base.
- **Value**: Efficiency gains through automation of document extraction, reconciliation, and advisory.

## 4. Microsoft Alignment
We leverage the **Microsoft FY26 Partner Playbook** to:
- Lead with **ERP Transformation with AI**.
- Utilize funded motions (**BAPA, ERP Accelerate, FastTrack**).
- Target **Business Applications** designation and specialized certifications.
