# BIR Tax Calendar

## Overview

This calendar tracks all Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR) filing deadlines for TBWA SMP.

> **Automated Reminders**: The BIR Compliance Bot posts daily reminders to #bir-alerts for deadlines within 7 days.

---

## Monthly Deadlines

| Form | Name | Deadline | Frequency |
|------|------|----------|-----------|
| 1601-C | Withholding Tax on Compensation | 10th of following month | Monthly |
| 1601-E | Expanded Withholding Tax | 10th of following month | Monthly |
| 1606 | Final Withholding Tax | 10th of following month | Monthly |
| 2550M | Monthly VAT Declaration | 20th of following month | Monthly (if applicable) |

### Monthly Workflow

```
Day 1-5:    Export data from Odoo
Day 6-7:    Reconcile and prepare forms
Day 8:      Manager review
Day 9-10:   File via eFPS
```

---

## Quarterly Deadlines

| Form | Name | Deadline | Period |
|------|------|----------|--------|
| 2550Q | Quarterly VAT Return | 25th after quarter end | Q1: Apr 25, Q2: Jul 25, Q3: Oct 25, Q4: Jan 25 |
| 1601-Q | Quarterly Income Tax | 15th of 2nd month after quarter | Q1: May 15, Q2: Aug 15, Q3: Nov 15 |
| 1701Q | Quarterly Income Tax (Individuals) | Same as 1601-Q | Self-employed |

### Quarterly Workflow

```
Week 1:     Export quarterly data
Week 2:     Prepare returns, supporting schedules
Week 3:     Manager and Director review
Week 4:     File via eFPS
```

---

## Annual Deadlines

| Form | Name | Deadline |
|------|------|----------|
| 1604-CF | Annual Information Return (Compensation) | January 31 |
| 1604-E | Annual Information Return (Creditable Tax Withheld Expanded) | March 1 |
| 1702-RT | Annual Income Tax Return (Regular) | April 15 |
| 1702-EX | Annual Income Tax Return (Exempt) | April 15 |
| 1702-MX | Annual Income Tax Return (Mixed) | April 15 |
| 2316 | Certificate of Compensation Payment | January 31 (to employees) |
| 2307 | Certificate of Creditable Tax Withheld | Within 20 days of year-end |

### Annual Workflow (January - April)

```
January:    1604-CF (Alphalist), 2316 (employee certificates)
February:   Audit prep, 2307 certificates
March:      1604-E (EWT Alphalist), ITR preparation
April:      1702 series filing
```

## Filing Methods

### eFPS (Electronic Filing and Payment System)

- **URL**: [https://efps.bir.gov.ph](https://efps.bir.gov.ph)
- **Used for**: Large taxpayers, mandatory e-filers
- **Payment**: Online through eFPS or authorized agent banks

### eBIRForms

- **URL**: [https://ebirforms.bir.gov.ph](https://ebirforms.bir.gov.ph)
- **Used for**: Non-eFPS taxpayers
- **Payment**: Manual payment at authorized banks

### Manual Filing

- Rarely used; only for special circumstances
- Requires RDO approval

## Penalty Reference

| Violation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| Late filing | 25% surcharge + 12% interest p.a. |
| Non-filing | 25% surcharge + 20% interest p.a. |
| Deficiency | 50% surcharge on deficiency amount |
| Fraud | Up to 50% of tax due |
