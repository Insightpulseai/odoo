Dataverse IT Consultancy is a Pasig-based consultancy that delivers analytics, automation, and AI-enabled operations — paired with a real Makati execution facility, W9 Studio, operated under the same business and TIN. ## 02 — Strategic Direction

### Vision, Mission & Objectives

**Vision**
To become the most trusted data + AI-enabled operations and creative delivery partner in the Philippines — where businesses and teams can reliably turn ideas into measurable outcomes.

**Mission**
To design and run analytics, automation, and production systems that help clients execute faster — combining business consulting with a real operating facility for content and deliverables.

### Objectives Across Five Domains

- **Customer:** End-to-end delivery from planning to production to reporting with consistent quality. Enable clients to launch content, campaigns, and communications without delays.
- **Market:** Position Dataverse as a differentiated operator: consultancy + studio + AI workflows. Build reputation as a "done-with-you / done-for-you" execution partner.
- **Financial:** Grow two complementary revenue lines: consulting/managed services and W9 Studio rental/production. Improve margins through reusable templates and tight scope control.
- **People:** Maintain a lean core team and partner bench across consulting, data/AI ops, production, editing, and admin. Keep SOPs and delivery playbooks for repeatable work.
- **Social:** Promote responsible AI use in business and media production. Offer accessible production options for SMEs and creators through a reliable, well-equipped studio space.

## 04 — Execution Framework

### Strategic Pillars

- **S1**: Two-engine business model — consulting + studio rental/production, both under one integrated operating system led by Dataverse.
- **S2**: W9 Studio as Makati execution hub — a real facility that converts plans into content and deliverables with professional workflow support.
- **S3**: Package-first monetization — standardized offerings for both consulting and rentals with clear inclusions, add-ons, and published rates.
- **S4**: Compliance-by-design — branch/permit alignment baked into billing and documentation to avoid rework and audit exposure.
- **S5**: AI-enabled throughput — automation and AI workflows to shorten production cycles and improve consistency across engagements.

### Programs

- **Program 01 (Consulting & Advisory):** Analytics, strategy, systems design, and business process optimization
- **Program 02 (AI Systems & Automation):** Workflows, reporting pipelines, agent operations, and AI infrastructure
- **Program 03 (W9 Studio Space Rental):** Hourly, half-day, and full-day studio rentals with flexible booking
- **Program 04 (Production Services):** Shoot support, editing, packaging, and content variant generation
- **Program 05 (Managed Services & Retainers):** Ongoing optimization, reporting cadence, and dedicated operational support on monthly or quarterly engagements
